Sticky Notes

learn more
Type your note in the input area and submit it by pressing 'Enter' or clicking 'Save'.
It will be kept in your browser's local storage,
so you can close this window, turn off your device etc.

All notes will stay there until you get rid one of them, by clicking 'Delete'
on the respective note or 'Delete All' button to clear all of them at once.

The app will recognize the language you set in your browser and
will save the date in the format in your preferred language.
